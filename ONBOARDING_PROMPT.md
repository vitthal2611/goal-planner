## Quick Setup Instructions

1. **Copy** one of the example prompts above (or create your own)
2. **Customize** with your specific goals, numbers, and habits
3. **Navigate** to the Goals tab in the app
4. **Create each goal** using the "Create New Goal" form:
   - Enter goal title
   - Enter unit (books, hours, km, etc.)
   - Set year
   - Click "Set Monthly Targets" to distribute across months
   - Click "Add Goal"
5. **Navigate** to the Habits tab
6. **Create each habit** using the "Create New Habit" form:
   - Enter habit name
   - Link to goal (select from dropdown)
   - Add trigger, time, and location
   - Click "Add Habit"
7. **Start tracking** in the Today tab!

## Need Help?

If you're unsure about your goals or habits, start with:
1. **One goal** in each life area (health, career, personal)
2. **Two habits** per goal (one morning, one evening)
3. **Realistic targets** (reduce by 30% if you're unsure)
4. **Review after 1 month** and adjust as needed
